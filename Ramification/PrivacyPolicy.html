Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2026

JoringYang ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Ramification mobile application (the "Application"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Application and the choices you have associated with that data.

1. Information Collection and Use

We only collect and use data that is strictly necessary to provide and improve the core functions of the Application (i.e., uploading images to our server for processing/display).

A. Device Permissions and Data

To use the features of Ramification, we may request access to the following features on your mobile device:

  • Camera: We require camera access to allow you to take new photos directly within the Application. Photos taken will be sent to our server based on your explicit action.
  • Photo Library / Storage: We require access to your device's gallery to allow you to select existing images to upload to our server.
  • Network / Internet Access: The Application requires internet permissions to fetch data from our backend servers and to transfer your selected images to our servers.

B. Image Data Processing

  • Explicit Action Only: The Application will never access your camera or gallery automatically in the background. It only accesses these features and uploads an image when you explicitly tap the upload button.
  • No Other Personal Data: We do not collect, store, or share any other personal identifiable information (such as your name, phone number, email, location data, or device identifiers).

2. Data Security

The security of your data is important to us. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your images while they are being transmitted to and processed on our servers. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

3. Third-Party Services

The Application does not integrate any third-party SDKs, advertising networks, or analytics tracking tools that collect or monitor your personal behavior and data.

4. Children's Privacy

Our Application does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
